My physicist dream

In my childhood, the books I liked most were series of Doraemon and Harry Potter, both full of fictions and marvellous fantasies. But there was another type I was also interested in, which is the story of scientific discoveries, and specifically, the story of theoretical physicists. What I did not realise is that MY TRUE INTEREST was on the process of reasoning, logicalising and philosophising. But not exactly the content of science.

In my teens, with good maths skills and interests on abstract theoies of physics, I grew fast on the tastes for physics and began to dream of being a physicist. And then I participated in CPhO(Chinese Physics Olympiad, derivative of The International Physics Olympiad). It was a memorable period. Although I didn’t get a high ranking.

It has to be claimed that I am quite a lazy person. Often thinking instead of doing, I like the style of reasoning or deducing but hate most parts of which combined with the experiments. All kinds of operating and I hate them all. Handicraft, Biology, especially operating with specific abstract knowledge and making experience links, such as Chemistry. That bothered me quite a lot.

And time passing by, with the period in College of Chemistry where my entire mentality had nearly been spoiled, I had to find another way of cheering myself up. It was then I astonishedly found my interests with physics were in fact the pursuit of reasoning, logicalising and philosophising. And it has nothing to do with physics itself although it contains lots of analogous ways of thinking indeed.

Then I realised that from the childhood and the very beginning of my life, it was to always my favorite to contemplate by oneself and to imagine the abstract and fiction rules in another world. I have never been attracted by the world but always fascinated by its latent patterns. So that I chose to be on the road of philosophy. To Develop sagacity and insight ,and look for some hints for who I am, and whom shall I become.

三年前的今天

三年前的今天，是我和丝儿第一次出去玩的日子。

按我们的风格，当然照旧还是去图书馆，不过她还带了一个同学。我们三个围着一张桌子各看各的书，其实很尴尬。

中午去吃饭就只有我们两个一起了。我胃不好吃不下去，虽然难受心里还蛮开心的。只要看着她吃饭和关心我的样子，心里就暖暖的了。

下午还是回图书馆，丝儿很困就趴在桌子上睡觉，我睡不着，偷拍了一张。动作大概就是这样。

那段日子虽然是青春的低谷，这天在记忆里却真是幸福的一天。
